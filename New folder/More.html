• 1. Documents & Communication:
      Passport (with valid visa, if required)
      Travel Insurance
      Flight/Accommodation Tickets (digital and printed copies)
      Copies of important documents (passport, visa, travel insurance, driver's license, etc.)
      International Driver's Permit (if necessary)
      Local SIM card or a reliable international roaming plan
      Emergency contact information (written down and stored digitally)
  • 2. Finances & Security:
      Cash (local currency and some USD for emergencies)
      Credit/Debit Cards (notify your bank of your travel dates)
      Travel Money Card (for added security)
      Money Belt or Hidden Pouch for valuables
      Small padlock for securing luggage
      Copy of your itinerary and important phone numbers
  • 3. Health & Hygiene:
      First Aid Kit (pain relievers, band-aids, antiseptics, motion sickness medication)
      Prescription Medications (with a doctor's note)
      Travel-sized Toiletries (shampoo, conditioner, soap, toothpaste, etc.)
      Sunblock and Insect Repellent
      Hand sanitizer and disinfectant wipes
      Reusable water bottle and water purification tablets (if necessary)
      Basic medical kit (including any personal medications)
  • 4. Technology & Entertainment:
      Phone and Charger
      Power Bank
      Camera and Charger (if applicable)
      Adapter/Converter (for international travel)
      Headphones or Earbuds
      Books, magazines, or e-reader
      Portable games or entertainment
  • 5. Clothing & Comfort:
      Comfortable Shoes (for walking and exploring)
      Versatile Clothing (layers for changing weather)
      Raincoat or Umbrella
      Hat and Sunglasses
      Travel Pillow
      Eye Mask and Earplugs
      Swimsuit (if applicable)
  • 6. Other Essentials:
      Backpack or Luggage (appropriate for your travel style)
      Packing Cubes (for organization)
      Guidebook/Maps (digital and/or physical)
      Language Phrasebook or Translation App
      Reusable Shopping Bag
      Small lock for securing luggage
      A sense of adventure and a positive attitude!
