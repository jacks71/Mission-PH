photographs, and musings from my adventures across the globe. Join me as I chase sunsets, sample street food, and lose myself in the vibrant tapestry of human experience.

Essentials for Every Traveler

  1. Documents:
      Passport (with valid visa, if required)
      Travel Insurance
      Flight/Accommodation Tickets
      Copies of important documents
  2. Finances:
      Cash (local currency and some USD for emergencies)
      Credit/Debit Cards
      Travel Money Card
      Money Belt or Hidden Pouch
  3. Health:
      First Aid Kit (pain relievers, band-aids, antiseptics)
      Prescription Medications
      Travel-sized Toiletries
      Sunblock and Insect Repellent
  4. Technology:
      Phone and Charger
      Power Bank
      Camera (if applicable)
      Adapter/Converter (for international travel)
